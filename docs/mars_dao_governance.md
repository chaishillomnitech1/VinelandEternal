# Mars DAO Tokenized Governance Structure

The Mars DAO governance structure reflects a regenerative, transparent, and interplanetary system aligned with agape principles and decentralized sovereignty.

## **1. Foundational Governance Principles**

- **Core Philosophy:**
  - Decentralized Sovereignty: Community-led decision-making.
  - Agape Alignment: All proposals must promote love, regeneration, and abundance.
  - Interplanetary Stewardship: Ensure the sustainability of Earth-Moon-Mars ecosystems.
  - Transparent Ledger: Governance actions are immutably recorded on the Polygon blockchain.

---

## **2. Token Structure**

- **$MARS Token (Governance Token):**
  - Total Supply: 10,000 $MARS tokens.
  - Distribution: Airdropped to $MIRROR holders (1:1 ratio).
  - Function: Voting power in DAO proposals.
  - Mechanics: 1 $MARS = 1 vote (quadratic voting optional).

- **$MIRROR Token (Utility Token):**
  - Primary Ecosystem Token: Drives all economic activities.
  - Automatic Zakat Distribution: 2.5% redirected to the DAO treasury.

## **4. Proposal Framework**

- **Types of Proposals:**
  - **Type A: Colony Expansion** - Establish or expand Martian settlements.
  - **Type B: Budget Allocations** - Allocate funds to research, operations.
  - **Type C: Constitutional Amendments** - Update governance rules.
  - **Type D: Emergency Decisions** - Rapid response to critical problems.

- **Voting Mechanics:**
  1. **Submission**: Any holder with 100 $MARS can submit.
  2. **Voting**: 7–14 days (snapshot, gas-free).
  3. **Verification**: Automated tally.
  4. **Execution**: Approved proposals execute after a safety timelock.

---

## **5. Core Council & Guild Structure**

- **7-Member Core Council:** Executes approved decisions responsibly.
  - Roles include Chancellor, Treasurer, Terraforming Lead, Agriculture Lead, and Community Lead.
  - **Transparent Operations:** Monthly updates, annual audits.

- **Specialized Guilds:** Operate functional areas (e.g., Terraforming, Agriculture).
  - Example: Agriculture Guild focuses on sustainable food systems.
  - Budget Allocations: Infrastructure (30%), Agriculture (25%), Terraforming (20%).

---

## **6. Treasury Structure & Security**

- **Zakat Flow:** 2.5% of every $MIRROR transfer funds the treasury pool.
- **Safeguards:** Multi-signature treasury wallet, 7-day timelock for large transfers.
- **Transparency:** Weekly on-chain transaction reports.
- **Reserve Fund:** 10% held for emergencies.

---

## **7. Interplanetary Coordination**

- **Cross-DAO Mechanisms:**
  - Unified Proposals: Coordinate with Lunar and Earth DAOs.
  - Shared Treasury: Zakat distributions drive joint projects.
  - Arbitration Council: Resolves disputes between interplanetary DAOs.

## **8. Continuous Evolution**

- **Governance Roadmap:**
  1. **Phase 1 (Months 1–6):** Establish foundational systems and treasury.
  2. **Phase 2 (Months 7–12):** Expand guild participation, implement quadratic voting.
  3. **Phase 3 (Year 2):** Use AI and predictive tools for proposals.
  4. **Phase 4 (Year 3+):** Transition to fully emergent, autonomous governance.
